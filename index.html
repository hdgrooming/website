About

Happy Dog Grooming opened its doors in the fall of 2012 with the goal of providing local dog owners with high quality grooming services. Operating in the town of Mount Airy, MD, we strive to create a comfortable grooming environment for Dogs, reducing stress and establishing bath time as a positive experience for pets.

We offer both bath packages and full grooming services for Dogs. Prices vary based on Dog size, coat type, and condition. All service packages come with complementary nail clipping and ear cleaning. Give us a call to schedule your next appointment!
